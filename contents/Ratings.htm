Ratings

The Ratings feature enables users to engage with the content with a rating as one method of providing feedback to the content owner.  Users can view the average rating score and number of times rated for both learning objects and playlists.  This is visible both via tiles and the details pages.

Viewing the rating

Clicking the star icon on a tile or from the details page open a the Ratings modal.  The overall average rating will be displayed.  Underneath it will also display the number of ratings submitted.

Rating a learning object or playlist

If the user has not rated the object, then when the modal is open the stars will be blank and a message will appear the item has not been rated yet and go ahead please rate the content.  Select anywhere from one to five stars to rate the item.  Once selected, the modal will refresh with the new average rating for the content along with the new total number of ratings.  Click the clear ratings button to remove the rating and refresh the ratings data.

Once the modal is closed the data will refresh on either the tile or details page from where it was launched.
