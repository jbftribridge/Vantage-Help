Improved Share Option

The share option has been improved so that learner can now share learning objects and playlists directly to an MS Team Channel or Team. 

When users click to share to a team or channel they will be directed to a page within the browser to search for a Team or Channel to share the link with.  They can also edit the default message to add more spefic detail to the message.

*At this time Microsoft Teams is the only social playform released to share directly to.

Users will click Share in the lower right hand corner to send the message.

 

Learners are also given the option within the modal to share the content via email. When users click 'Generate Email' the share link will be presented in the default email client configured by the computer's operating system.

 

Finally using the third option, by clicking the copy to clipboard icon the URL will be saved so that it can be pasted wherever the users wishes to paste it.

Previously learning object shares were limited to email output and Playlist shares were limited to copying share links.
